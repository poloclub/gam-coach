Introduction

Hello! We are a team of computer science researchers. Thank you so much for taking your time to help us improve our tool, GAM Coach.

You might have heard that some banks are using Artificial Intelligence (AI) to decide whether a person can or cannot get a loan. These banks ask for the loan applicant's information, such as annual income, employment length, and credit score. Then, the banks use AI programs to go through all collected information and decide on whether to approve or reject this client's loan application.

Unfortunately, many people's loan applications are rejected by these programs. Therefore, we are developing GAM Coach, an interactive coaching tool to help these rejected loan applicants succeed in their future applications! To do that, GAM Coach identifies an applicant's current weakness and provides simple and customizable strategies to get their next loan application approved.

In this task, we want you to pretend to be a person whose loan application is rejected by a bank. The bank points you to GAM Coach to help you improve and succeed in future applications.

It is very important to try your best to imagine that you are a real user—using GAM Coach to find the strategies that you think are useful. Our goal in this study is to learn what strategies would be helpful for real users and how GAM Coach can help everyday people.
